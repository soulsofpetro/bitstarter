Looking around in your place you'll find stuff lying around you don't actually need or use anymore. Selling it wouldn't bring the money it is actually worth. Instead you just keep your stuff, unused and taking up space in your appartment. Well, how about that: Give the things you don't need anymore to someone looking for just the things you want to get rid of. Now you might ask, 'What's in for me'? Well, first let's say that you've contributed in the preservation of our natural resources. Every product not beeing bought is one pile of garbage less polluting our environment. Last but not least, there is something called karma:)

You might ask yourself, 'who decides who gets my free stuff'? We've created a virtual currency. Any object you want to give away has a certain value depending on how many people would like to have it and the amount of the virtual currency they're willing to 'pay'. So this means, before you get something, first you have to give. By giving you receive a certain amount of a virtual currency, which in return you can spend on something you'd like to have.
